Essential Oils

What Are Essential Oils?

Essentails Oils are made from plant extracts which makes them extremely concentrated. Essential Oils are used for a varitey of things, though they're mainly used for aromatherapy. These oils can have many benefits which is why so many people like to use them as an alternative to medicine.

Ways To Use Them

  • Essential oils can be diffused
    • In a diffuser or in hot water.
    • On a cotton ball or swab.
    • Smell straight from the bottle.
  • Essential oils can be applied topically
    • The oils should be diluted before appling to skin.
  • Essentials oils can be ingested
    • This can be dangerous because the oils are so strong.
