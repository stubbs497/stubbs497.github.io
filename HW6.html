Libra Astrology Sign

September 23 - October 22

Libra is seventh Zodiac sign out of the twelve Zodiac signs. Libra is one of the air signs, along side of Aquarius and Gemini. This sign is represented by the scale, and is the only sign to be represented by an object. The ruling planet of Libra is Venus. Libra is represented by the traits of balance, compassion, diplomacy, and morality to name a few. Libras are most compatable with Aquarius, Gemini, and other Libras. The opposite sign of a Libra is Aries which is known for boldness.
